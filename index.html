Welcome!

I am a third-year PhD candidate at the University of North Texas, specializing in the analysis of large-scale networks using high-performance computing.

My research focuses on dynamic networks, which capture the real-world dynamics of systems. I design parallel algorithms to update important properties in time varying networks. Additionally, I am creating realistic benchmarks for dynamic network algorithms.

I am interested in applying deep learning techniques to graph analytics, specifically in the context of dynamic graphs, to uncover complex features in networks. My goal is to contribute to the field of dynamic network analysis and develop effective algorithms and tools for understanding complex systems.
